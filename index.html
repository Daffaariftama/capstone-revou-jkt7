About the Capstone Project

This capstone project focuses on addressing the decline in bicycle sales in Australia
by analyzing existing data to enhance annual sales in the region.

Business Understanding

We are concentrating our analysis on Australia, which has experienced a significant decline in bicycle sales. This indicates an opportunity to boost sales in the country. Our primary goal is to identify effective strategies to increase bicycle sales in Australia by 5% annually.

Background

The "Bike Sales Europe" dataset contains detailed data on bicycle sales across Europe. This data is used to analyze monthly and yearly sales trends in Europe. From our analysis, we observed that bicycle sales in Australia have significantly declined compared to other European countries.

Problem Statement

How can we increase bicycle sales in Australia by 5% per year?

Objective

The goal of this project is to boost bicycle sales in Australia by 5% each year. This project involves in-depth market analysis, identification of consumer preferences, and visualization of sales trends tailored to the Australian market. The expected outcome is a significant increase in market share and revenue from bicycle sales in Australia.
